Welcome to Hypo Scan

About Hypo Scan

Hypo Scan is an AI-powered brain imaging and monitoring solution designed to detect, analyze, and track brain activity in real-time. Our cutting-edge technology leverages deep learning and neural analysis to assist in the early detection of neurological conditions, hallucinations, and mental health disorders.

We aim to revolutionize brain health diagnostics by integrating artificial intelligence with medical imaging to provide precise, real-time insights. Hypo Scan helps researchers, doctors, and individuals gain a deeper understanding of neural activity and cognitive states.

Our mission is to advance neurological research, improve mental health assessments, and create a future where AI-driven brain monitoring enhances lives.

Our Services

Hypo Scan offers advanced brain imaging and monitoring solutions powered by AI and deep learning.

  • Brain Image Analysis: AI-driven detection and classification of brain scan abnormalities.
  • Hallucination Detection: Real-time EEG and fMRI monitoring to identify hallucination patterns.
  • AI-Powered Diagnostics: Machine learning models for predictive brain health assessments.
  • Neural Activity Tracking: Continuous surveillance of neural signals for mental health insights.
  • Remote Brain Monitoring: Cloud-based access to brain scan reports and activity logs.
  • Medical Report Generation: Automated summarization of brain scan results for clinicians.
